Elendel Chamber of Commerce

The purpose of the site will be for the residents of Elendel to visit the site and get information about upcoming events in the area, press releases and updates about current local concerns, and to see what projects and goals are planned for helping the community grow and progress.

Misson Statement: The purpose of the chamber is to establish relationships in Elendel and the surrounding cities, to help promote business and community prosperiety and to see the success and unity of the greater basin.
