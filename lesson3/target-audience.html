Targert Audience

Persona 1

Germain Hasting

  • Owner & CEO Hasting Motorcar Services
  • 55 years old
  • Married
  • 2 kids
  • 1 grandson
  • Focused on continuing to build his business to leave behind a legacy for his family. Wants to see the success of Elendel and would be happy to expand his business to the surrounding cities.
  • Spends his days going over ledgers, making sure that his fleet of vehicles is maintained, and ensuring that he has competent trained staff to operate and maintain his livelihood.
  • He has a nice manor left to him from his father and he risked his wealth and inheritance to diversify and get into the new invention that is the automobile. He has a vested interest in the goings on of the city and surrounding areas to make sure nothing will undermine his business.

Persona 2

Larsa Drascol

  • Bank Supervisor
  • 40 years old
  • Married
  • No children
  • Ambitious and wanting to move up in the banking world. Constantly seeking new opportunities that will propel her career forward.
  • Spends her days going through the broadsheets (newspapers) looking for stories that might hint at things that the bank would want to invest in. New technology, property that might be built, budding sports teams and others things of interest. Her husband finds her hobby to be eccentric but endearing.
  • She lives in a no nonsense townhouse with little to maintain. She is particularly fond of the area that she lives but she does feel that there could be more going on. Perhaps more opportunities to mingle with her neighbors and see if they know about any ventures that her bank might want to invest in.

Scenerios

  • Larsa wants to see if there are any community events happening soon that she will be able to attend where she will be able to meet some new people. She is curious about what types of events might be happening and what the date and time of the event will be.
  • Germain goes to the website to see what news there is of the surrounding cities and what opportunities or markets will be opening up for him to potential expand.
  • Germain uses the website as a business owner to keep up on knowing what city bills and ordinances are being proposed and passed that might effect him and his business.
  • Because the site posts relevent city news, after a big city catastrophe, a young mother gets on the site to get information about what happened and how it impacts the city and what resources are available to those who are impacted.
  • An aspiring business owner goes to the site to get information about what it takes to start a new business.
